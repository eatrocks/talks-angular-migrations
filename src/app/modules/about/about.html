About this App


This is a task manager application used to illustrate how to migrate an AngularJS application to the new Angular framework. Click the 'Tasks' menu item to display the list of tasks. To create one, you must log in (no real checking of passwords is done at present ... that is left to you as an exercise).

You can complete tasks (click the checkbox) or toggle your own tasks between being private/personal to being public. You can only complete your own tasks or public ones. You cannot delete or update tasks which you did not create.

To update a task that you own, simply click it and it's text will display for editing in the entry field below the task list. To update the text, simply change it and press enter. To cancel editing and enter a new item, simply click the item again to unselect it.
